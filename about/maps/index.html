Pearl Hacks Travel

Here is all the information you need to make it to Chapel Hill on April 2nd - 3rd, 2016.

Location and parking

Pearl Hacks will be held in Genome Science Buildings on the campus of UNC Chapel Hill. The address of our venue is:

250 Bell Tower Road
Chapel Hill, NC 27599

Free parking is available within easy walking distance of Pearl Hacks beginning at 5:00pm Friday afternoon if you choose to arrive early.

  • Bell Tower Parking Deck
  • Cobb Parking Deck
  • Venable Parking Lot
  • Nash Parking Lot
  • FedEx Global Education Center Parking Deck

Carpools

All carpool reimbursements have to be pre-approved.

A carpool consists of 3 or more students intending to participate in Pearl Hacks.

Once you receive registration information, e-mail us at hello@pearlhacks.com with subject line "Carpool Reimbursement". List the names of participants who will carpool together and which schools, city and state you will be traveling from. You may be asked to submit reimbursement documents prior to or after the event in order to receive your travel grants. Qualifications for carpool grants are as follows:

  • RSVP via link in your email
  • Drive yourself and at least 2 passengers to Pearl Hacks
  • Make sure everyone checks in (even if you have to come late)
  • Submit a project

We cannot make exceptions to this policy. Limit one per carpool.

Large Vans

Contact us if you are looking to coordinate a van of 6 or more. Email hello@pearlhacks.com with the subject title "Large Van Carpool". The process of receiving your grant is similar to normal carpools.

International travel

We love international participants! Unfortunately, Pearl Hacks cannot provide assistance for international travel or visa paperwork. However, if you do make it to Pearl Hacks, send us an e-mail with subject line: "International Participant". The transportation team would love to show you around Chapel Hill if time permits!

Chartered Buses

Pearl Hacks will be sending buses that will be making stops at the following locations:

  • NY - NC: New York University -> Rutgers University(NJ) -> Howard University(DC) -> University of Virginia(VA) -> UNC Chapel Hill
  • MD - NC: University of Maryland at College Park(MD) -> UNC Chapel Hill
Bus seats are allocated for the first 48-56 participants who signed up and indicated that they will be traveling from the states we are providing buses for. You need not be students from universities the buses will be making a stop at. If you signed up late, don't worry. Typically, not all participants who signed up would go and chances are there will be bus seats available for you. We are currently organizing buses for Georgia and Florida participants. The route we are planning for the bus is University of Florida(FL) -> Georgia Tech (GA) -> UNC Chapel Hill. Please stay in tune for updates.

Other transit options

Flight reimbursements are very limited. Unfortunately, we will not be providing flight reimbursements for people from states we are providing buses to. If you do require a flight reimbursement, please send us an e-mail at hello@pearlhacks.com with subject line: "Flight Reimbursement". State the city and state you will be traveling from and attach a link to a 30-second video or a 150 words max response on what interests you about computer science and what you hope to do with it in the future! Someone from the transportation team will reach out to you within a week, and you will have another week to submit your flight receipts. You will also be required to submit an additional of 2-3 forms in order to receive grants from us. Due to our limited budget, we are very unlikely to reimburse more than $150 per person for flights. However, we will review flight reimbursements on a case-by-case basis and we will try our best to help you out!

Questions?

If you have any questions please email us at hello@pearlhacks.com.
